Hi, I'm Seth

I am a UX designer, and I strive to fix the World’s problems.

My Skills

User Research

My biggest task while researching is to feel what the user feels and try to get them to say why they feel how they do. I love to repeat the question, “Why”.

Prototyping

I put the users needs first. What do they want to see, find, and do while they are on this product. I check everything I do over many times, as seen in my NASA renovation

User Testing

While users complete tasks I have given, I want them to give feedback on other things they may have noticed. The key to understanding how they feel is encouraging them to speak freely while testing.

Non-profit Company Desktop Makeover
